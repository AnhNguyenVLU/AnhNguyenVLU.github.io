About

I am a lecturer at the Faculty of Information Technology, Van Lang University, and Assistant to the Dean. I earned my BSc in IT from VLU (2018–2022) and my MSc in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence from the Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Thailand (2022–2024).

My research sits at the intersection of machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision, with active threads in LiDAR point cloud processing, recommendation systems, medical image segmentation, Vietnamese sign-language recognition, and agricultural vision. I supervise undergraduate research and thesis projects at VLU and collaborate with peers across partner institutions.

Research interests

  • Computer Vision

    Object detection, segmentation, LiDAR / point clouds, medical imaging.

  • Deep Learning

    CNNs, Vision Transformers, hybrid pruned-residual architectures.

  • Recommender Systems

    Image-based recommendation, fusion models for behavior data.

  • Applied ML

    Healthcare, agriculture, sign language, mobility, networking.
